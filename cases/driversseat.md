# Driver's Seat Cooperative

## Description

Driverâ€™s Seat [0] is a a driver-owned, independent cooperative that provides an app to enable ride-hailing drivers and on-demand delivery drivers to collect work data to answer questions such as: When is the most productive time to work? Which is the best platform? What is the best driving strategy for you? They aim at providing workers with actionable insights to increase their wages.   
Furthermore, they sell the collected data to cities and transportation agencies to address planning, government and policy issues (DaaS) [1].
